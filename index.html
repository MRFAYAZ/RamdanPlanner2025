Ramadan Schedule


"Ramadan is not just about fasting.It's about purifying the soul and seeking closeness to Allah"

Tasks to do

"Ramadan is a month of immense blessings, where even the smallest good deed is rewarded manyfold"

  • Quran Recitation
  • Dua & zikr
  • Learning and understanding 2 hadiths from Sahih Bukhari Daily
  • Taraweeh(8 or 20)
  • Fastings
  • Namaaz 5 times + Salat ut taubah before sleeping two rakat + Tahajjud

1.Quran Recitation

30 chapters for 30 days complete a chapter in a day by reading it in before and after 5 salah's

2.Dua & zikr

Include these duas in your dua

  • Rabbana Aathiq Riqhabana Minan Naar (Meaning: "O Allah free our necks from hell fire")

  • Allahumma Ballighni Lailatul Qadr (Meaning: "O Allah, bless us throughout this month of ramadan and let us reach the night of decree(lailatul qadr))

  • Rabbana Taqabbal Minna innaka Antas Samee'ul Aleem (Meaning: "O Allah, accept [this] from us indeed you are the Hearing, the Knowing)

    4.Taraweeh

    Taraweeh is a Sunnah prayer that is performed at night during Ramadan.
  • Women can pray Taraweeh at home or at the mosque.
  • How to pray Taraweeh
    • Perform the Fardh and Sunnah of Isha prayer Set the intention to pray Taraweeh Pray the first four rakats of Taraweeh in sets of two rakats Take a short break after each set of four rakats Repeat the process until you reach the desired number of rakats End with the Witr prayer
